Project Lilith

Not All Hell Is Scorching In Fire

The choice is yours, but in the end it does even matter

Project Lilith is an upcoming webcomics series that follow the journey of a young woman from the destruction of the life that she grew up with and the identity that she held onto to so tightly, to her fight to prove that she is more that what her blood is telling otherwise

The choice is to fight is hers, but is it going to be enough or is she forever destined to succumb to the same fatee
